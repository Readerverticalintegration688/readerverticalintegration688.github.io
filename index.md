## 📊 Understanding Your Connection

The dashboard provides clear data for your session. Use these metrics to track your performance:

- Ping: This number shows the time in milliseconds it takes for data to reach the server. Lower numbers indicate better performance.
- Jitter: This number shows how much your ping changes during play. Low jitter results in smooth movement.
- Packet Loss: This shows how much data fails to arrive. Zero percent is the goal.
- ISP Logs: The software tracks changes in your internet service provider performance. Use these to verify that the route remains stable.
